Meet the Chefs!


With over 50 years of cooking experience we present you India with a fresh new look, with a creative menu offering a blend of contemporary and traditional home favorites, a home kitchen straight from the heart of a mother - daughter duo.

It all started with experiments with food at home to kitty parties to wider functions for extended family and then swiftly growing the dream into a reality aka our own restaurant! We always aim to bring a fresh, creative, nutritious, tasty, and a balanced meal. We are progressive with our cuisine and not bound by the 'same-old' mentality as we are the Change- makers and by the service of our food we are breaking the shackles of misogyny, serving and treating everyone with equality with a bigger goal of building a better world.
