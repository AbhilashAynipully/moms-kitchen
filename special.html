Main Course : Veg Biriyani

Vegetable biriyani is an aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, mix veggies, herbs & biriyani spices.


Biriyani is a celebration of exotic flavors and pleasing aromas infused into a pot of goodness.This dish is a perfect dinner packed with fragrant rice, vegetables, crunchy nuts, aromatic spices and herbs.

Dessert : Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a sweet confectionery or dessert, originating in the Indian subcontinent and a type of mithai popular in India.


It is made mainly from milk solids, traditionally from khoya, which is milk reduced to the consistency of a soft dough. Modern recipes call for dried or powdered milk instead of khoya.
